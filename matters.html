Why Reducing Plastic Matters

Plastic waste is a big problem for the environment. Here are some simple reasons why reducing plastic can help.

It Takes Hundreds of Years to Break Down

Most plastic stays in the environment for a very long time.

It Harms Animals

Animals can mistake plastic for food, which is dangerous for them.

We Can Make a Difference

Small changes, like using fewer bottles, help reduce waste.

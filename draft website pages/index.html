Easy Plastic-Free Swaps

Here are some simple changes you can make to use less plastic.

Plastic bottles → Reusable bottle

Use a refillable bottle instead of buying water bottles.

Plastic bags → Tote bag

A tote bag can replace single-use plastic bags.

Coffee cups → Reusable cup

Bring your own cup when you buy coffee.

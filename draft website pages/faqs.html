Recycling Questions

Some common questions people have about recycling and plastic waste.

Does my recycling really get recycled?

Recycling rules are different in each area, but clean and sorted materials are more likely to be recycled.

Do I need to wash containers?

Rinse out food and drink so the items are not dirty before you put them in the recycling bin.

Can I recycle any plastic?

Not all plastics are accepted. Check the symbols on the packaging and your local recycling guide.
